Why do i code

I was never deeply interested in technology. Words and languagues on the other hand fascinated me from my earliest childhood. Why? Common saying is that languague is the closest thing to spells and magic we modern humans have left. Just say or write down any word and that's it, you just put vivid images and ideas right into someone's brain without any possibility for him to stop it. Magical, isn't it? So as it happens my interest in coding began on one rainy dain in Spain when i accidentaly misclicked on Wikipedia and went from spanish characters to programming characters. Now that's odd, i told myself. I know they are named programming languagues, but i always thought about them as consisting of numbers and cold math. Is coding just another way of communication? Just with computers instead of people? The idea felt completely amusing, i had to know more. First i read articles about coding, then youtube tutorials, then i paid for an online course and then another and another. With every single piece of knowledge i acquired i felt the need to go deeper and deeper. The magic simply never went away, and i haven't looked back since.
